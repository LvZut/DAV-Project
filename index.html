# DAV-Project Data Analysis and Visualization Grading criteria project: *Process* (20%) --- To what extent have you followed a rigorous design and development process and have you worked with others to enhance your and their learning? The process will be graded on evaluation of rigor as evidenced in your process book, and on the cleanliness of your repository and commit history (commit every day!). *Final report* (40%)--- To what extent have you clearly documented your project, the analysis, and your understanding of the chosen methods? The report will be graded on axes of clarity and thoroughness. *Final website demo* (40%) To what extent does your website give insight into to the chosen dataset? Is it a complete story and is it useful for your intended audience? The website will be graded primarily on insight, usefulness and coherence.
